'We're all gonna die in a few decades anyway.'



Ever had thoughts like these run through your head?


If so, you aren't alone.


Climate change is having a devastating impact on the mental health and emotional wellbeing of people, especially young people, around the world.

Many people see no future for themselves or their family, and feel a sense of hopelessness in the face of certain doom.


As we try to tackle our looming environmental crisis, it's imperative that we also tackle the mental health crisis lurking in its shadow- climate anxiety, or "ecoanxiety".


Building resilience to anxiety and doom starts with you and your community!


On this site, you'll find the latest articles and reports from reliable institutions about the growing problem of climate anxiety. You can also connect with professionals and experts trained to help you along the way from paralysis to meaningful action.


When a friend or family member is struggling with their feelings about the state of the world, it's hard to know what to say. Talking about the future is a harder conversation to have than ever, but it's just as important as it's always been, if not more. Through a simple chatbot on our Start the Conversation page, you can practice helping someone through their feelings of doom, and along the way, learn about how to work through these feelings yourself.
